WHAT IS THE TEDxUCSI UNIVERSITY THEME FOR THIS YEAR?

The theme for this year is "Reading Beyond the Line". Oftentimes, we view the world as inherently black or white, good or bad, leaving little room for the in-betweens, or what goes beyond our own perspectives. Reading Beyond the Lines challenges us to question assumptions, uncover hidden meanings, and discover the deeper truths that shape how we think, feel, and understand.
